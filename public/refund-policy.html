Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Thank you for partnering with Cyber Enterprises for your digital growth, web design, and ad campaign optimization needs.

1 Service Delivery & Execution Track

All web development services, search engine optimization setup, and social media ad placements are custom-built per project milestone agreements. Work begins immediately upon receipt of the initial retainer deposit or invoice authorization.

2 Cancellations

Clients can choose to halt recurring marketing retainers by providing a 15-day written notice to our support team. Any work hours or server architectures deployed prior to the cancellation date will be billed pro-rata.

3 Refund Policy

Because our agency delivers custom professional hours, cloud resource setups, and custom digital marketing configurations, all payments made for activated service cycles or completed web layouts are strictly non-refundable. If a project is cancelled mid-milestone, the client is only responsible for settling the work executed up to that timestamp.
